Stabby Wood

LEVEL: 13
HP: 410
spirit
This vengeful tree can quickly break off its own branches to impale its opponents. Its rapid growth results in infinite replenishment allowing it to fight forever if not defeated properly. Additionally, its size proves beneficial to its offensive and defensive ability, adding even further longevity. It's rumored that when this wood isn't busy stabbing, it is left undisturbed and will mysteriously bloom with ghostly flowers. Yet, none have ever been recovered. The Black Spider on its face will leave the Stabby Wood following defeat and immediately seek out another. Travelers often track the Spider in hopes of finding the Ghost Flowers, however many never return alive or do so empty-handed.
