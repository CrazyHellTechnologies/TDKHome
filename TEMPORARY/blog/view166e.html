The Dusk of Time

By Draconid_Jo
POSTED: 08 Feb 2021 08:59
BUMPED: 14 Mar 2023 08:16
CATEGORY: Poetry and Lyrics
FEATURED: Yes (yaynikki)

Our hopes and dreams, keep shining on like solar beams...

Wish upon a star, that we'll make it there someday, though it always seems so far away, know that somehow we will find our way...

The sun sets in the evening sky, we keep chasing after its light...

We hope, hope and we pray, somehow some way we'll make it to that light someday...

We keep chasing our hopes and dreams, no matter how far off they all may seem...

It will be alright: someday we will make it to the light...



Our hopes and dreams, no matter how dark things may seem...

Wish upon a star, that we'll make it through these days, even though we think this is the end, know that somehow we will find our way...

The sun sets in the evening sky, we all hope it will rise again...

We hope, hope and we pray, somehow some way we'll make it through these dark, dark days...

We keep chasing that ray of light, even into the deepest darkest night...

It will be alright: someday we will make it to the light...



Our hopes and dreams, no matter how dark things may be...

Wish upon a star, that we'll make it through all this, even though we know this is the end, know that somehow we will find peace...

The sun sets in the evening sky, we all know it will never rise...

We hope, hope and we pray, somehow some way we'll get to see the light again...

We keep chasing our hopeless dreams, no matter how pointless it all may seem...

It will be alright: today we'll all make it to the light...
