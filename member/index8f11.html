Biography

Just a bit about me. I enjoy writing and history, one of my favourite pastimes is writing historical research papers. I primarily study the Second World War and Ukrainian history from 1917-1933. My primary area of focus has shifted over the years and right now I primarily study war crimes and the psychology of the people who commit them. I have also begun in-depth research into the Vietnam (American) war and am getting back into studying the Great War as well. A constant background study I conduct is about the foreign volunteer legions of the Waffen SS, truly a fascinating topic.

I assist in the operations of an antique business that primarily deals with art and militaria from the 1939-45 period and also personally collect items from the period.

I have a PC, Xbox and Switch, I don't play games as much as I used to, but have found time again to enjoy them. I've been really enjoying Dragons Dogma, lately.

If you enjoy talking about history, feel free to shoot a message my way as it's one of the few things I have a genuine passion for if you couldn't already tell.

For anything else, feel free to ask me any questions.
