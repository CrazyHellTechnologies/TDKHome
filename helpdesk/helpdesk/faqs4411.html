How do I change my username?
Note: You must be a member for at least a year for the option to be available.

Here are the steps to changing your username:
1. Go to Settings, and scroll down to the section called "Change Username".
2. Enter the new username twice, and click Save.
3. Within a moment, you will receive an email with a 6-character verification code (good for 24 hours).
4. Go back to Settings, and scroll down to the same "Change Username" section.
5. Enter the verification code, and click Save.
6. All set! (No need to log out)
