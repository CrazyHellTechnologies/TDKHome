A Song of Life

By Draconid_Jo
POSTED: 09 Mar 2022 01:59
BUMPED: 14 Mar 2023 08:16
CATEGORY: Poetry and Lyrics
FEATURED: Yes (yaynikki)
(Go to comments)

I just want you to live! And I don't want you to die!

As I watch you fade away, it brings tears to my eyes...

And so I sing this Song of Life: my prayer for you to live...

But a prayer is only words... Are words all that I can give...?

And every day that I'm not here, I wish I was with you...

Though my love for you is blind, at least its also true...

And so I sing this Song of Life: my prayer for you to live...

May God give you new life, 'cause words are all that I can give...

Oh, how time can be so cruel! I don't want you to die!

When I see you in this state, it makes me want to fight!

And so I sing this Song of Life: my prayer that you'll be healed...

But can I win this fight? Or are all my hopes unreal...?

But it is you that saved my life, a debt I can't repay...

So I cannot just stand by, and watch you fade away...

And so I sing this Song of Life: my prayer you'll be revived...

I owe my life to you, and it'd kill me if you died...

But do any of them care?! They left you here to DIE!

As I watch you fall apart, they all just live their lives...

And so I sing this Song of Life, 'cause my love for you is true...

But one weeb can't save your life, and neither can a few...

But did any of them care?! They all just walked away!

If they hadn't made that choice, then things wouldn't be this way...

And so I sing this Song of Life: my prayer for you to live...

Only God can save you now, and time is all that we can give...
