Might of Dark Mite of Light

By IAMMICHAEL
POSTED: 18 Feb 2023 23:29
CATEGORY: Poetry and Lyrics
FEATURED: Yes (Draconid_Jo)
(Go to comments)
The abyss
Of my soul
Is a deep
Dark well

My face a mere
Reflection
Of what lurks
Deep within

A sadistic freak
A reaper of blood
A ghoul of stagnant mush
A lover of None

My heart
Made of Iron
My blood
Of Ice

I am a beast
I am a child
I am evil
Shot with love

Two separate
Fragments
One from Hell
One from Heaven

A liar
A lover
A helper
A shell

One side is
From outside
The other is
Deep in

But now I am
Broken
Cracked like
The Liberty Bell

Evil is
Winning
And I will
Raise Hell
