DSiPaint Spriter

DSiPaint Spriter is the HTML5-canvas painting application built just for the Nintendo DSi. It allows members to paint artwork to save to PNG cel images for sharing. Depending on the configuration of the save, it can be 4 frames, 8 frames, or 4 wide frames.
